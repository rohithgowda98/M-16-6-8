Food

Food is any substance consumed to provide nutritional support for an organism. Food is usually of plant, animal, or fungal origin, and contains essential nutrients, such as carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, or minerals.

Pizza (Italian: [Ëˆpittsa]) is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, baked at a high temperature.

A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo. Margherita pizza is a classic option topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, and mozzarella.
